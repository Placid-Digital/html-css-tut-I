They used an Interactive voice response (IVR)  System for solving customer problems. Nowadays It was transferred to chatbot which will show our website menus as option tour customers. Once you get 100 or 1000 visitors to your website on a daily basis you can make a decision to choose a button-focused chatbot.

2) Keyword Recognition focused Chatbot

I visited a supermarket that has more variety of grocery products. I asked one salesperson where potatoes are for sale. He guided me to which row and rack stored vegetables. Here “potato” is a word that recognizes the human and replies relevant to that rack. When it comes to the computer, it recognizes the keyword and replies to the relevant webpage link to your customer. Once your website gets 100 to 1000 returning visitors to your website on a daily basis you can make a decision to keyword recognition-focused chatbot.

3) Conversion focused Chatbot

One month ago I bought groceries for my home with my wife’s grocery list in a nearby grocery shop. This month, I went to the same shop to buy groceries and used the same grocery list. Finally, it saved more time to get my groceries home. When it comes to the website, you need to ask the question “Can I repeat your old order which you made for the previous month? “. If they replied “yes”, We can proceed with your order for the existing customer. Once your website gets 100 to 1000 conversion which means sales of your products or service to your website on a daily basis you can make the decision to Conversion focused Chatbot.

It is classified into a number of channels which are categorized as online and offline Marketing channels.

Online Marketing  channels :

This includes Search Engine Optimization, Email, Social Media, Affiliate, Mobile and Video Marketing, Content Marketing, Website Marketing, and Pay-Per-Click Advertising.

Offline Marketing channels:

This includes social media such as TV, Radio, as well as SMS and Bill Board Marketing.

 

REASONS HOW DIGITAL MARKETING PLAY AN IMPORTANT ROLE IN BUSINESS:

1.    COST EFFECTIVE

Digital Marketing helps in developing small businesses as well, with a better cost-effective marketing channel that delivers good results. 2.    BRAND REPUTATION

3.    ERA OF MOBILE CONSUMER

4.    IMPROVED ACCURACY AND EFFICIENCY

5.    BUILDS TRUST

Digital Marketing leads a path to a successful business through a strong marketing strategy.
