     Keywords in SEO (Search Engine Optimization) are the ideas or phrases which define what the content is all about. They are the shortcuts which summarize the entire article. Keywords help Search engines like Google to search for appropriate queries.

WHY KEYWORD RESEARCH IMPORTANT FOR SEO

Keyword Research is a process to identify popular phrases or words which people frequently search using search engines.  Researching the keywords will help content writers or marketers to understand better to create optimized SEO content. It is one of the important SEO factors that improve the rank of Google and also adds value to the website.

1.    KEYWORDS ACT AS HINTS

Keywords have the power to express both explicit and implicit information. For example, the keyword “safest car” has explicit information stating “safety concern” as well as implicit information stating “who owns a car for the first time”. Researching such powerful keywords and implementing the content will add value to the blogs. To start with the keyword research it should include the idea, topic or head keyword which is also called “seed”. The seed comes from the respective industrial knowledge related to the products or the services we provide. This seed increases the search volume as well as it will be helpful to find more related keyword suggestions.

 

2.    TO CREATE AN OPTIMIZED CONTENT

    Search engines like Google will be changing its search algorithm multiple times a year. Maintaining the SEO strategy regularly will always keep our blog in top searches ahead of the competitors. Change of keywords is also important in content optimization to meet the current demands. While changing the keywords, it is important to follow the SEO best practice to change the Meta description and subtitles that match with the updated content. Search Engines always looks for consistency in the entire content. Such Keyword Research practices will enhance the visibility of the SEO content as well it helps in creating an optimized SEO content

 

3.    PROMOTION OF BRANDS OR SERVICES

Performing the separate Keyword search in specific, under different categories of particular brands or products will lead to higher chance of attracting the targeted client leads who look for the exact kind of products listed under the particular category.

 

4.    TARGETS CUSTOMER

     The important factor of keyword research involves understanding the audience and the queries they ask, to answer them effectively related to the content with a combination of phrases and keywords.  Effective keyword research will automatically satisfy the customer requirements related to the particular product or services thus meeting their demands on time.

 

5.    SITE ARCHITECTURE

     When a website needs to be redesigned, keyword research helps in editing the existing site architecture also recreating the new website by copywriting. Generally, the site architecture is built only after the first round of keyword research is performed.

Keyword Research plays a vital role that helps in streamlining Search Engine Optimization (SEO) campaign and also brings the targeted users to visit frequently which in turn produces profitable results. A proper Keyword research will result in a successful Internet campaign.

            Search Engine Optimization is an analytical, technical and creative process to increase the online visibility of a website through search engine results. It is a process of optimizing a website where users can easily search through search engines. To start with the SEO content we need to create an outline first based on our understanding of the article.

 HOW TO WRITE AN SEO OPTIMIZED CONTENT

1.    KEYWORD RESEARCH

For better SEO content, we need to understand which keywords to be used in the Google search to see what kind of contents are currently in ranking. Keywords which are searched frequently will drive towards the competition for the premium position. It is always best to do research on keywords before the content is written.

2.     TECHNICAL NEEDS

To create optimized content, knowing a few things technically about how search engines like Google handle the pages will assist in building a strategy. A good understanding of how a blog should be structured will be more efficient in creating an optimized content.

3.    CONTENT-LENGTH

                 Generally, longer content and articles are preferred by search engines, so that users can search easily. Longer content adds more value to the customers and also increases visibility.

4.    CHOOSING TITLE

            The Title plays an important role in content.   Choosing the appropriate title related to the content is also an important factor to consider when a user searches using the search engine.

5.    ADDING LINKS

The Inclusion of External and Internal links adds more value to the content. Internal links refer to a post within the article and External links refer to a post outside the article referring to the authority websites like CNN, Wikipedia which helps the users to explore more on the particular topic which will create a positive impact to the users. Also, the URL of the content should have a focused keyword which will look more professional and likely to be shared more on the social media channels.

6.    VISUAL CONTENTS

Including images and videos will further attract the users and also the time spent by the users will also be increased on the site.

 7.    ADDING META DESCRIPTION

Meta Description is a quick summary of the article that is included below the title. Adding a Meta Description to the content helps the users to grasp easily about what the content is all about.

 8.    SOCIAL SHARING BUTTONS

Use of social sharing buttons provides users a platform to share our content thus increasing the brand exposure as well as improving the user experience.

 9.    MOBILE-FRIENDLY

    Since Mobile marketing is growing rapidly all over the world, blogs created should be useful for mobile users as well which will serve for future needs.

The main focus of the SEO content should be more informative and attractive which will be optimized for search as well as conversions and the next focus should be targeted towards the blog content which demonstrates how to use products and providing links relevant to the posts will automatically drive the users to spend more time in the blog.

The answer is, SEO means search engine optimization technique, the goal is to fetch a place for a website in the first page of search engine.

Let me tell you few qualities that a website should imbibe to become SEO Friendly-

Usage of keywords – Proper inclusion of all necessary keywords in the website is one important aspect so that when users type a particular keyword in search engine it should root to that particular websites which contain information on that specific keyword. While website designing, one should think about all the words that a user may use to search information and include all those words in the content of the website.

This can be achieved by building a proper hierarchy.

Control Hacking – Regular checks on the website should be performed to find out hacked data and those should be removed here and there. Also eliminating user generated spam should be done as soon as possible.
